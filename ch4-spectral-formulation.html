4. Spectral Formulation

The Spectral Formulation recasts the entropic field in terms of its spectral modes, revealing a deep connection between entropic geometry and eigenvalue structures. By decomposing the entropic field into spectral components, this chapter shows how stability, resonance, and pattern formation emerge from the underlying entropic dynamics. Readers interested in functional analysis, operator theory, or quantum analogies will find this chapter particularly rich, as it hints at a spectral unification of geometry, information, and dynamics.
