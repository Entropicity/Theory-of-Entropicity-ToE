8. Literature Studies

Literature Studies situates ToE within the broader intellectual landscape of entropy, geometry, and information. Rather than functioning as a conventional thesis‑style literature review, this chapter traces conceptual lineages: from classical thermodynamics and statistical mechanics to information theory, entropic gravity, and information geometry. It highlights where ToE aligns with prior work, where it diverges, and where it extends existing ideas into a new ontological register. Readers gain a historical and conceptual map of the territory ToE inhabits and transforms.
