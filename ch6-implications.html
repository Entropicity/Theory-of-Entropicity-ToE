6. Implications

The Implications chapter explores what ToE means for our understanding of physics, cosmology, and ontology. It examines how an entropic substrate reshapes familiar concepts such as causality, locality, emergence, and the arrow of time. Readers are invited to consider how ToE might reinterpret gravitational phenomena, quantum behavior, and information flow as expressions of entropic geometry. This chapter is both speculative and rigorous, mapping the conceptual horizon opened by the theory.
