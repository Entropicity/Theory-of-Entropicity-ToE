References

The References section gathers the works that inform, contrast with, or are challenged by the Theory of Entropicity (ToE). It includes foundational texts in thermodynamics, statistical mechanics, information theory, geometry, and modern theoretical physics, as well as relevant philosophical and mathematical sources. For readers wishing to situate ToE within the broader scientific and intellectual tradition, this section serves as a curated gateway into the surrounding literature, as well as an opportunity for advanced future works and research.
