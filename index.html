Welcome

This is the official archive of the Theory of Entropicity (ToE), a foundational framework first formulated and further developed by John Onimisi Obidi, which positions entropy not as a derivative quantity, but as the primary ontological substrate from which geometry, fields, information, and physical law emerge.

What This Site Contains

This repository organizes the conceptual, mathematical, and expository structure of the theory into a coherent, navigable archive. Each section of the site corresponds to a major component of the framework:

  • Concepts — Core definitions and philosophical foundations
  • Equations — Mathematical structures and field equations
  • Notes — Development notes, drafts, and expository essays
  • Diagrams — Visual and geometric interpretations
  • Papers — Links to formal publications and academic archives
  • Books — Long‑form works, compilations, and extended treatments of ToE
  • Monograph — The canonical, structured presentation of the Theory of Entropicity (ToE)

The Vision

The Theory of Entropicity (ToE) is not an extension of existing frameworks — it is a new foundation. It proposes that the universe is an entropic manifold whose structure and evolution arise from gradient-driven ontodynamics. This site preserves the canonical formulations of the theory and anchors its multi‑stage multi‑phase diffusion pipeline (MSMPDP) across public platforms and academic repositories.
