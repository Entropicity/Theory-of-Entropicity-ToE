9. Comparative Frameworks

In this chapter, ToE is placed side‑by‑side with major theoretical frameworks such as General Relativity, Quantum Field Theory, statistical mechanics, and various entropy‑based approaches. The comparison is structural rather than merely historical: it examines differences in foundational assumptions, mathematical architecture, and explanatory power. By contrasting ToE’s entropic ontology with metric‑based, probabilistic, or Hilbert‑space‑based frameworks, this chapter clarifies what is genuinely new about ToE and why it cannot be reduced to a variant of existing theories.

A rigorous comparison with: GR, QFT, statistical mechanics, holography, entropic gravity, information‑theoretic physics
